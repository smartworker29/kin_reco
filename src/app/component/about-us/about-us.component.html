What is Kin?

Kin Parenting's mission is to use the power of technology to make parents' lives easier. We believe that parents anywhere should be able to quickly discover trusted information and plan for their family without spending hours pouring through endless search results, social media comments and filling up spreadsheets. That's why we are building Kin, a discovery and planning platform for parents -- to simplify parenting

Which cities does Kin cover?

Kin is in beta. It is currently available only in San Francisco Bay Area. We would like to get initial feedback and hone our offerings before we take on the world! Thank you for your patience. Please mail us at contact@kinparenting.com to get notified when we expand to your city.

In what other format is Kin available?
