How do I interact with Kin, the chatbot?

Kin is still learning and growing everyday. Here are some of the things you can try with Kin on Messenger.

Hi/Hello/Menu - When you want to wake up Kin or restart the conversation
Events - To list events
Places - To list camps
Camps - To list camps
Help - Brings up help message
Add kid - To add a kid's age and interests
Saved Events/Saved Places/Saved Camps - To list your saved items
Subscribed - To list upcoming events from your subscribed (favorite) places
Sunday - To list events on Sunday or any other day of the week
March 15 - To list events on a particular date
Feedback - To provide your feedback or report any issues
